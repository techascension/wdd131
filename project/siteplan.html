Website Plan for "Strength Through Christ: Embracing Emotional Resilience"

Site Name

Site Name: StrengthThroughChrist.org

This name reflects the core mission of the website, emphasizing the strength and emotional resilience that can be found through a relationship with Christ.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to be a resource for individuals seeking emotional resilience through Christ. The site will provide gospel teachings, inspiring talks by church leaders, practical self-care guidelines, and a community forum for sharing experiences.

Scenarios

  • How can I find strength during difficult emotional times through scripture?
  • What resources does the church provide for mental health and well-being?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #4A90E2 (used for headings)

Secondary Color: #D35400 (used for subheadings)

Background Color: #f9f9f9 (used for the overall page background)

Typography

Headings: Arial, sans-serif (used for H1 and H2)

Body Text: Arial, sans-serif (used for paragraphs)

Quotes: Italicized for special emphasis within body text
